MERCHANT OF VENICE - VISUALIZATION INSIGHTS

Dashboard Charts:
1. Monthly Revenue Trend - Shows business growth over 2011
2. Customer Segments - Distribution of customer value tiers
3. Top 10 Products - Best performing items by revenue
4. Revenue by Country - Geographic performance analysis
5. RFM Scatter Plot - Customer clustering by behavior

Key Insights:
- Revenue Spikes Toward the End of the Year indicating heavy seasonal demand
- Product Sales Are Power-Law Distributed. a few products generate the largest revenue
- Best month: November
- Top customer segment: Champions
- Geographic concentration: most customers come locally from the UK
